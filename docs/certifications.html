Certifications

Our school's Technology Track focuses on getting students a vast amount of Professional Certifications in a variety of fields, so that they are ready for anything that comes their way. Certifications offered include all of the certifications in the IC3 suite, the Microsoft Specialist Series, the CIW Web Foundations Associate Series, and the Adobe Suite. These certifications range from photo editing, video editing, server management, web development and so much more.
